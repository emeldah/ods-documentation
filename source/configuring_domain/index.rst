Configuring a domain
====================

In this section are listed and documented all features available in Configuration section of the back office of an OpenDataSoft domain. The Configuration section contains all kinds of options and configurations for security (i.e. anonymous portal access authorization, signup authorization, SAML management), legal information (i.e. cookies, terms and conditions), tracking, social networks and reuses (i.e. social networks integration, comments activation and reuse submissions authorization), dataset catalog and processing, and domain URL.
